If you order from our site, you can know what your order status is at any given moment.

The dishes are prepared one after the other. Each dish has a different preparation time. After placing the order, you can tell how much time is left for each dish until it is ready in real time.

Each extra per dish will increase the preparation time by 2 minutes and add 5₪ more.

You can see all the dishes you ordered under the 'My Order' tab. In addition, you will see the preparation time and the total price.

The information on each dish you ordered will be updated in real time. The information includes: price, extras and preparation time.

Once your order is ready, the shipment will leave for your home and you will be able to see the messenger location in relation to your home in real time.
